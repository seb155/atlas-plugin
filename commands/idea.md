# /idea — Strategic idea intake & vision alignment

Invoke Skill `atlas:vision-alignment` with the provided arguments.
Evaluate a new idea against the mega plan, sub-plans, features, backlog, and product roadmap.
6-step workflow: CAPTURE → SCAN → ALIGN → MAP → DECIDE → TRACK.

**Usage**: `/idea "description of the new idea or feature request"`

ARGUMENTS: $ARGUMENTS
